Facts

Seaspiracy claims that fishing takes 2.7 trillion fish from oceans globally each year. If this rate of fishing continues, the oceans will be “virtually empty” by 2048, according to marine biologist Dr Sylvia Alice Earle.

46% of the Great Pacific Garbage Patch is Made Up of Fishing Nets. Seaspiracy says that one of the greatest threats to our oceans is not from plastic straws- which make up about 0.03% of plastic entering our oceans- but fishing nets. Taking into account that the Great Pacific Garbage Patch is 1.5 million sq kms in size, this is an astonishing amount of fishing nets left in our oceans.
